## The 4 golden rules
1. **Folder name = the package slug exactly** — all lowercase, hyphens, no spaces, no capitals.
2. **File names exactly as listed below** — rename your phone photos (e.g. `IMG_6345.jpg` ➜ `d3-1.jpg`). Lowercase, hyphens.
3. **Only .jpg / .jpeg / .png / .webp** — never HEIC (iPhone photos must be exported/converted to JPG, which also resizes them smaller).
4. Day photos are **auto-counted**: upload as many as you like per day, just keep them in sequence (`-1, -2, -3, …`) with no gaps.
